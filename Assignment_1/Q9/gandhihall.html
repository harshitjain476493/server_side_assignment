        6. Gandhi Hall, Indore
        Gandhi Hall, Indore
        Gandhi Hall | #6 of 7 Historical Places in Indore
        
        When you make a list of places to visit in Indore, 
         forget to include the Gandhi Hall, 
        the most prominent historical building in the city. 
        Built in 1904, the building features the 
        Indo-Gothic style of architecture. The clock tower, 
        minarets, and domes of the structure add to its grand appeal.
        
        The building was initially named King Edward Hall and 
        was renamed as Gandhi Hall shortly after Indiaâ€™s independence. 
        Currently, it serves as the town hall of Indore and many cultural 
        and art exhibitions are held here.
        
        Trivia: Guess who inaugurated the Gandhi Hall back in 1905? 
        None other than George V, the then Prince of Wales!
        Location: Mahatma Gandhi Road
        Timings: 10:00 am to 6:00 pm
