        5. Kanch Mandir, Indore
        Kanch Mandir, Indore
        Kanch Mandir | #5 of 7 Historical Places in Indore
        
        Kanch Mandir aka the Temple of Glass is not just a religious place, 
        but also one of the top historical places to visit in Indore. 
        This famous Jain temple, built around 1903, boasts of a history of 
        over 100 years. The place was constructed by Shri Seth Hukam Chand, 
        who was an industrial pioneer and renowned leader of the Jain community.
        
        The exteriors of the temple look like a medieval mansion and feature a 
        shikhara and a canopied balcony. The interiors, however, are fully decorated
        with mirrors, Chinese glass lamps, and glass panels. And thatâ€™s where the 
        temple gets its name from. The structure of the temple and its ornamentation 
        feature medieval and old Jain style. The temple also has the idols of the 
        Thirthankaras installed inside it, with mirrors placed on both sides.  
        
        Trivia: Artisans were brought in from Jaipur and even Iran by 
        Shri Seth Hukam Chand to complete the construction and ornamentation of the temple.
        Location: Itwaria Bazaar
        Timings: 5:00 am to 12:00 pm and 4:00 pm to 8:00 pm
        Entry Fee: Free
